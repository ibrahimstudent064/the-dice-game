# 🎲 Pig Game (Dice Game)

A classic two-player dice game built with vanilla JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. Players take turns rolling dice, accumulating points, and trying to be the first to reach 100 points.

## 📖 Game Rules

1. **Roll Dice**: Click the "🎲 Roll dice" button to roll a die
2. **Accumulate Points**: The number rolled is added to your current score
3. **Roll a 1**: If you roll a 1, you lose all current points and your turn ends
4. **Hold**: Click "📥 Hold" to add your current score to your total score and pass the turn
5. **Win**: The first player to reach 100 points wins the game!

## 🚀 How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Player 1 starts the game
3. Click "🎲 Roll dice" to roll
4. Continue rolling to accumulate points, or click "📥 Hold" to save your current score
5. Avoid rolling a 1, or you'll lose your current points!
6. First player to reach 100 points wins
7. Click "🔄 New game" to start over
